Information about your health

If you have a disability or health condition we need to:

  • understand how it could affect your ability to carry out the tasks of your job
  • consider adjustments we could make to help you
  • be confident that your attendance record will be good

What we need to know about your health

We need to know about any:

  • adjustments you think you might need
  • health conditions that could affect your work
  • absences in the last 2 years that lasted more than 16 days
  • health issues you might want to discuss with an occupational health provider
You can save your progress at any time and come back later to give us the rest of the information.
