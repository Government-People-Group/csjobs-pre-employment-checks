Your pre-employment checks

The Civil Service requires all successful applicants to go through pre-employment checks. If your pre-employment checks are completed successfully you will receive a formal offer for the job role.

The position you’ve been offered needs:

  • a Disclosure and Barring System (DBS) check

! Warning Pre-employment checks can take a long time. We will show you how you can check the progress of your checks once you've given us all the information we need.
